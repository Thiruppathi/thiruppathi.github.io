2*100 Days of Reading

After successfully completing Round 1 of #100DaysOfReading, here I am celebrating the success of Round 2 of #100DaysOfReading.

Rules

  • Read book atleast 20 minutes a day
  • Track the progress through Bookly & Goodreads
  • Read more on weekends
  • Don't allow other commitments affect your reading streak
  • 100DaysOfReading shouldn't affect Job Search & other learning activities(Certifications/MOOCs/R&D)

Challenges

In Round 2, I read less(11 Books, 83 Hours) compared to Round 1(23 Books, 115 Hours). I had too much to handle in this last 100 days & had to endure a period of hardship. I picked books mostly for pleasure reading; mainly because I was already consuming lot of Technical/Non-Fiction content as part of my job search (Thats another story for another time).

The only Non-Fiction book I've picked was How to solve it by George Polya, which really helped in the interview preparation & gave a chance to introspect how I approach problem solving; I was also maintaining the reading streak without compromising interview preparation. All other fictional books helped me to overcome the burnout feeling.

Overall I am glad that I maintained a model of well balanced reading diet. So far the only diet I'm capable of following.
